About Us

Milon is an artificial-plants brand that offers a curated collection of home décor items — from the sparkle of art-deco pieces to the calm minimalism of modern Scandinavian design.

Many of our pieces are thoughtfully designed and crafted in collaboration with skilled Indian artisans.

Our aim is simple: to make your house feel like your home.
